Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for your rafting business is to effectively market your services, engage with customers, streamline the booking process, and build a reputable brand that attracts adventure seekers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our target audience for the water rafting website comprises adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers seeking thrilling experiences in natural settings. These individuals are typically aged between 18 and 45, with a moderate to high disposable income, and a passion for adrenaline-pumping activities. What do they want? They value exploration, excitement, and connecting with nature, often prioritizing experiences over material possessions. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Our target customers are tech-savvy, active on social media platforms, and often seek out new and exciting adventures to add to their bucket list.
